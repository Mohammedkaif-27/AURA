PHILIPS REFUND POLICY

1. Refund Eligibility
Refund is allowed only under the following conditions:
- Product is damaged on delivery
- Product is defective and cannot be repaired
- Wrong product delivered
- Product not functioning within first few days of use

2. Refund Window
Refund window depends on product category:
- Philips OTG HD6976/00: 7 days
- Philips Party Speaker TAX5206: 7 days
- Philips Car Thermal Box TB5101: 7 days
- Philips SPA8000B Speakers: 7 days

3. Refund Conditions
Refund will be approved only if:
- Product is returned in original condition
- All accessories are returned
- Serial number is intact
- Invoice is available

4. Non-Refundable Cases
Refund will NOT be approved if:
- Product is physically damaged by user
- Product is burnt due to voltage issue
- Product repaired by unauthorized technician
- Warranty period expired

5. Refund Mode
Refund is processed to the original payment method after verification.
