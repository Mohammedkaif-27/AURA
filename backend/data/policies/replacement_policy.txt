PHILIPS REPLACEMENT POLICY

1. Replacement Eligibility
Product is eligible for replacement if:
- Product received is defective
- Product stops working within the replacement window
- Product has major functional issues verified by service team

2. Replacement Window
Replacement allowed within:
- 7 days from purchase for Philips OTG HD6976/00
- 7 days from purchase for TAX5206 Speaker
- 7 days from purchase for TB5101 Thermal Box
- 7 days from purchase for SPA8000B Speakers

3. Post-Replacement Window
After replacement window expires:
- Product is handled under Warranty Service
- Repair will be done free of cost (if under warranty)

4. Replacement Conditions
Replacement request requires:
- Purchase Invoice
- Serial Number validation
- Product inspection result
- Full accessory return

5. Not Eligible for Replacement
Replacement will NOT be provided if:
- Product is physically damaged by user
- Product tampered or opened
- Unauthorized repair attempt
- Misuse or commercial misuse
