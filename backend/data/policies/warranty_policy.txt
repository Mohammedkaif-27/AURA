PHILIPS WARRANTY POLICY

1. Standard Warranty Coverage
All Philips consumer electronic products are covered under a limited warranty period starting from the original date of purchase.
- Philips OTG HD6976/00: 2 Years Warranty
- Philips Party Speaker TAX5206: 1 Year Warranty
- Philips Car Thermal Box TB5101 / TB5301: 1 Year Warranty
- Philips SPA8000B 5.1 Speaker System: 1 Year Warranty

Warranty covers:
- Manufacturing defects
- Hardware malfunction under normal usage
- Internal component faults

2. Warranty Does NOT Cover
- Physical damage
- Liquid damage
- Burnt components due to power surge
- Unauthorized repair or modification
- Consumable parts such as batteries, cables, external body parts

3. Proof Required
To claim warranty, customer must provide:
- Valid Purchase Invoice
- Product Serial Number
- Customer Details

4. Service Resolution
If defect is verified:
- Product will be repaired free of cost
- If repair is not possible, replacement decision is taken as per policy
- Service turnaround time depends on service center availability

5. Location
Warranty is valid only within the country of purchase.
